My Approach to SEO

Four focused steps from market research to honest reporting.

Research and Compete

I start with your competitors, not your website. I identify who's ranking at the top of your market and reverse engineer exactly why - their site structure, the pages driving their leads, the keywords they're targeting, and the gaps they're leaving open. This tells me what's already working in your market before I touch anything on your end.

Tools

  • SEMrush
  • A Ahrefs
  • SF Screaming Frog
  • Google Search

Audit and Strategize

Once I know what the market looks like, I dig into your current site. I find what's already ranking, protect it, and identify what's holding you back. From there I build a keyword-driven roadmap - organized around how your customers actually search - so every page we build or improve has a clear purpose and a target.

Tools

  • Google Search Console
  • SEMrush
  • SF Screaming Frog

Build and Optimize

This is where the work happens. Clean site structure, dedicated service and city pages, technical fixes, on-page content written around your target keywords, strategic internal linking, and full Google Business Profile optimization - all executed in the right order so nothing cancels each other out.

Tools

  • SF Screaming Frog
  • Google Search Console
  • W WebCEO
  • LF Local Falcon

Track and Report

I track your keyword rankings, organic traffic, and GBP visibility every month. You get a clear, straightforward report - no SEO jargon, no vanity metrics. Just what moved, what it means for your business, and what we're focused on next.

Tools

  • Google Search Console
  • AA Agency Analytics
  • LF Local Falcon
  • SEMrush

FAQ

Straight answers on scope, timelines, and how I handle projects.

What SEO services do you offer?

I specialize in SEO and WordPress development for local service businesses and agencies. That covers local SEO, on-page optimization, Google Business Profile management, and WordPress SEO depending on what your site and market actually need.

How much does hiring a freelance SEO specialist cost?

Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your project. I don't do one-size-fits-all packages. The best way to figure out what makes sense is to get on a quick call and go from there.

What does your SEO process look like?

I start by understanding your business, your market, and what's already working before touching anything. From there we move into research, strategy, execution, and ongoing optimization so every decision has a reason behind it.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

For SEO, you're looking at a minimum of 3 months to start seeing real movement, with full campaign support running up to 9 months. Timelines are always discussed upfront based on your goals, so nothing is set in stone until we talk.

How do payments and revisions work?

I require a 50% deposit before work begins, with the remaining balance due at agreed milestones or upon completion, all processed through Wise. Revisions are unlimited, because the goal is to get it right.
